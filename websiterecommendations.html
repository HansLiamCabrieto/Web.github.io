Our Gaming Recommendations

Stay updated with our recommendations about games.








Here are our top PC games of 2025, this is purely based on personal preference.

(This list is not in order)


Valorant

Valorant is a tactical first-person shooter game developed by RIOT games, a game where you are placed in one of two teams to attack or defend, or a free for all deathmatch to see who is the best at killing other players, a game fun with friends. You will either LOVE it or HATE it, maybe even both

Genshin Impact

Genshin Impact is an open-world RPG developed by HoYoVerse, a game where the player wanders around the world completing quests, meeting new characters, defeating enemies, exploring dungeons/domains for artifacts that boost their stats, the game follows the story of the main character in search of their lost sibling. A game that requires time and effort, very grindy, but also fun.

Roblox

Roblox is a sandbox game founded by David Baszucki and Erik Cassel, a game where your only limit is your imagination, looking for horror games to play? Roblox has it, a game that needs time and effort? Roblox has it, or something dumb and simple? Roblox has it, or do you want to create your own game? In Roblox, its a simple feat. A multiplayer game that is fun to play alone and with friends.

Minecraft

Minecraft is a survival game created by Mojang. This is a game where you can play singleplayer or multiplayer and explore a randomly generated infinite world, consisting of dungeons, temples, caves, mineshafts and more, while also creating endless amounts of contraptions, houses, buildings with no limit to how you create them. When you get bored of standard Minecraft, you can add mods to your servers by downloading them online, enchancing and giving you new experiences.

Elden Ring

Elden Ring is an extremely challenging action RPG game created by FromSoftware. In this game, the players have to explore and fight many bosses to become the Elden Lord and complete the game, a game where mistakes are punished harshly, where you must learn or die. A game made for players who crave challenges and the joy of completing it.
